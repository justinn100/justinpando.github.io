ABOUT ME

Graphic Designer/Student

My name is Justin, I am a graphic designer who since the age of 16 I was very interested in design in general. Since high school I always designed my own clothes like shoes, shirts or jackets. The people around me praised me a lot for what I did, until one day I decided that I wanted to do this not only as a hobby but as something professional. From that moment I began my journey in design. For me, creativity is expressing the ideas that go through my mind from day to day. I have big dreams like design for major fashion companies and even being able to design my own clothing brand. My dream is just staring.
